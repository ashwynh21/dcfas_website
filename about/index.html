About Us - The Debt Counsellors & DCFAS

Debt Counselling is a solution for over-indebted borrowers that ensures sufficient protection against loss of assets and assists with the restructuring of debt so that the consumer can meet his / her basic living expenses. Debt counsellors negotiate with your credit providers on your behalf to arrange an affordable repayment amount

How DCFAS works to help

STEP 1

Submit your details to us using our online contact form to get a phone call from one of our bright, switched-on consultants on the same day, during working hours. Only to be guided step-by-step through the rest of our quick and easy application process over the phone and via email.

STEP 2

First, your consultant will establish how much your income and expenses are on an average monthly basis. If you spend more than you make this means that you are over-indebted and qualify for debt review.

STEP 3

Next, we'll let all your credit providers and the credit bureaus know that you are entering debt review with DCFAS. As follows, your credit providers will not be able to take legal action against you for 60 days.

STEP 4

After asking your credit providers to send over a certificate of balance (COB) for all your accounts. We will draw up a reorganized payment plan for you.

STEP 5

Then, we'll negotiate with your credit providers to have your monthly instalments and interest rates reduced. We'll ensure that you can afford to cover your monthly living expenses, as well as stick to your more manageable reorganized debt repayment plan.

STEP 6

Our specialist attorneys will apply to the magistrates court to have your reorganized debt repayment plan declared a court order that is legally binding. Your debt counsellor will be the applicant on your behalf, so you will not have to appear in court.
